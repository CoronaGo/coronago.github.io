A service to connect volunteers to vulnerable people stuck in home quarantine

CoronaGo is a service to connect volunteers to vulnerable people stuck in home quarantine & help get essential goods delivered to their doorstep in a safe manner

Learn More

The problem

As the number of reported cases rises globally,we are soon approaching stage 3 of the panademic in our country where people are forced to stay quarantined in their homes to avoid Community spread.Government infrastructure would need assistance in finding people in quarantine,their needs and their needs and their location

Volunteers

A healthy induvidual with no active symptoms can sign up as a volunteer.

Requester

A person in quarantine can sign up and post a request of essential items they need and their location.

Adaptable

Volunteers can read details of each request present in his area and accept one that they can fulfill

Mutual Matching

Once accepted,the request moves from active active state to pending state.

Privacy

Once the volunteer accepts the request,they are given the contact details of the quarantined person and they are both have a chat open in the app.

Flexible

They can then discuss further details of the delivery like time and exact location.
